Transforms
==========

Dataloader-stage transforms (torch-only). A complex-aware analogue of
:mod:`torchvision.transforms` covering magnitude / phase extraction,
spectral FFT operations, spatial reshaping, polarimetric-SAR channel
selection, and statistics-based normalization.
